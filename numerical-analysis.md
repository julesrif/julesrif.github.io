This module introduces key aspects of mathematics and statistics as they apply to data science and artificial intelligence. Students build a foundation for analysing and solving problems across both disciplines, starting with the core mathematical and statistical concepts underpinning AI and data science. Topics include foundational mathematical principles, the application of quantitative and computational techniques, and the interpretation and presentation of numerical results to support evidence-based decision-making.

## Learning Outcomes

1. Understand and apply foundational mathematical principles and methods relevant to data science and AI.
2. Critically evaluate statistical methodology and its application within data science and artificial intelligence.
3. Apply programming and data-driven techniques to solve numerical problems from both theoretical and practical perspectives.
4. Interpret and present numerical results effectively to support decision-making processes.

---

## Key Artefacts

- **Collaborative Discussion:** Statistical methodology evaluation and its application in AI-driven data analysis.
- **Programming Exercises:** Applying computational techniques (Python) to solve numerical problems - regression, optimisation, and probability modelling.
- **Reflective Practice:** Bridging mathematical theory with practical AI/data science applications.
