This module explores Artificial Intelligence as a multi-disciplinary subject aimed at developing systems that model human intelligence. The course covers essential concepts, principles, and ethical frameworks required for AI professionals, emphasising the importance of data in driving business insights and decision-making.

## Learning Outcomes

1. Understand the legal, ethical and professional issues brought up by AI and the impact of AI on society.
2. Understand and critically analyse the essential concepts, principles, methods, techniques and problems of AI.
3. Demonstrate a critical understanding of data requirements and programming paradigms applicable to AI.
4. Apply and evaluate critically the various methods, tools and technologies applied to an AI project.

## Key Artefacts

- **Collaborative Discussion:** Ethical implications of autonomous decision-making in AI systems.
- **AI Project:** Design and evaluation of an AI-driven solution addressing a real-world business problem.
- **Reflective Practice:** Critical evaluation of AI's societal impact, balancing innovation with accountability.
