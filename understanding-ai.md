This module explores Artificial Intelligence as a multi-disciplinary subject aimed at developing systems that model human intelligence. Given the ubiquity of AI and the explosion of 'big data', the course covers essential concepts, principles, and ethical frameworks required for AI professionals. Students engage with current and future developments in the field, gaining both theoretical knowledge and practical skills to design solutions for contemporary challenges.

## Learning Outcomes

1. Understand the legal, ethical and professional issues brought up by AI and the impact of AI on society.
2. Understand and critically analyse the essential concepts, principles, methods, techniques and problems of AI.
3. Demonstrate a critical understanding of data requirements and programming paradigms applicable to AI.
4. Apply and evaluate critically the various methods, tools and technologies applied to an AI project in order to develop an effective plan and delivery of solutions to a business problem.

---

## Key Artefacts

- **Collaborative Discussion:** Ethical implications of autonomous decision-making in AI systems and the boundaries of machine agency.
- **AI Project:** Design and evaluation of an AI-driven solution addressing a real-world business problem, covering data requirements, methodology selection, and delivery planning.
- **Reflective Practice:** Critical evaluation of AI's societal impact, balancing innovation with accountability.
